# Abiotic Stress Mechanism Index

This file organizes plant abiotic stress physiology by stress type, primary limitation, mechanistic response, measurable traits, and research interpretation.

The goal is to provide a research-centered framework for designing, analyzing, and interpreting plant stress physiology experiments.

---

## General stress physiology framework

Abiotic stress responses can be organized into the following biological sequence:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Abiotic stress exposure] --> B[Primary physical or chemical limitation]
    B --> C[Stress perception]
    C --> D[Signal transduction]
    D --> E[Physiological response]
    E --> F[Biochemical and molecular adjustment]
    F --> G[Growth and developmental trade-offs]
    G --> H[Yield, quality, survival, and recovery outcome]
